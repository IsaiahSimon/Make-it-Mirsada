What I Do

I love to inspire my clients with my paintings and drawings. I am a freelance art instructor, and experienced host for "Paint & Sip" networking events.

I'm open to host a class at your next event, party, or get together. All of my paintings are for sale, and I'm currently accepting commissions for custom artwork.
